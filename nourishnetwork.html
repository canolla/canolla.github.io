Nourish Network

Brand Development

UX/UI

Web Development

For one of the projects in year-two of my degree, I was tasked with creating a social enterprise. My response was Nourish Network, a service which provides a place for distributors to redistribute their surplus produce through connecting them to local communities. This involved a full brand identity, along with a dynamic website and a campaign launch timeline with collateral and an opening event.
